at work, school, personal lives, or big names/creators of products/companies. We think the mountain climb was easy for them, and it should be easy for us.

We all know people at work, school, personal lives, or big names/creators of products/companies. We try to think about what they know and all the greatness they have achieved. By doing so we paint a very false picture. We think the mountain climb was easy for them, and it should be easy for us.

To make things even more complicated, our field is filled with advancing technologies and there are always these great people that we learn about. We look at people around us learners of things old and new and think “these guys and girls know what they’re doing.” I “should know what Im doing”
